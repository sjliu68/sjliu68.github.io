News

October 2019: I joined the HKU Light Pollution ECF Team as a Research Assistant at The University of Hong Kong.

July 2019: I started working at OneSpace Technology in Chongqing as a Remote Sensing Engineer.

June 2019: I received my bachelor's degree in Geographical Information Science from Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou.

March 2019: Our journal paper on land use and land cover mapping by integrating object-based regularization and ConvNets using optical and PolSAR data was available online.

February 2019: We achieved top 2% (18/1329) in preliminary and top 3% (29/1329) in semifinal in the Alibaba Cloud German AI Challenge 2018 for Remote Sensing Scene Classification of Local Climate Zones.

March 2018: Our work about using active learning to reduce the need of labeled samples for remote sensing image classification was accepted to IGARSS 2018.
